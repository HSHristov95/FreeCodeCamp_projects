Tomato-sauce egg recipe

This is a recipe for eggs in tomato sauce. It is quick and easy to make. Perfect to prepare, when you don't have much time for cooking

Ingridients:

  • 5 or 6 eggs;
  • 1 tomato can;
  • Sunseed oil;
  • Parsley;
  • 1 onion head;

Instructions:

  1. Mix the eggs in a bowl;
  2. Chop the onion into small cubes;
  3. Cut the parsley into small pieces;
  4. Mix the onion and parsley in the tomato sauce;
  5. Add 1 soup spoon of sunseed oil to the mix;
  6. Put the eggs in a pre-heated pan;
  7. After 5 minutes, pour the mix in the pan;
  8. Cook on small heat for 15 to 20 minutes;
  9. Serve and enjoy!
